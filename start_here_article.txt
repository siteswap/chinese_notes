If you want to quickly improve your comprehension of spoken Chinese, it's a good idea to start with the most frequently used words first. Did you know that the 60 most frequent words cover half of all spoken Chinese?

So: 
	1) Click the 'Word List' button, or click the high-lighted words
	2) Click 'Add to Deck' button to create spoken word flashcards
	3) Click 'Study Now' to train your skills on these words

When you're ready, you can start adding your own content. So, if your goal is to follow casual conversation, then TV series dialogue from opensubtitles.org could be a good learning resource. Or perhaps your italki.com lesson notes.

The tool will identify words most relevant to your learning, as well as the most helpful phrases to learn these words in-context with relevant lexical chunks and collocations. It will then create spoken word flashcards so you can train your ear, and use spaced repetition ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spaced_repetition) so that you don't forget.
