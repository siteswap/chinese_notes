First get lots of listening exposure, so you are familiar with hearing ~150 first words. *Then* learn the rules. Don't expect to learn the rules, and like programmed machine, be able to produce the sound. Language learning is not like learning maths or engineering.

Tricky initials:
zh - similar to the 'dge' in judge
r - closer to 's' in measure than to english r
少 vs 小 （sh vs x)
钱 vs 天 (q vs t)
一次 vs 意思 (c vs s)
在 vs 菜 (z vs c)
往 vs 玩
高 vs 狗
谁 vs 水
房间 vs 饭店
会 vs 回 // only the tones differentiate them
